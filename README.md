# Product Requirements Document: EconNews - Economic Development News App

## Problem Statement
Economic analysts need a reliable, centralized platform to access real-time news and data on local and global economic development. Current news sources are scattered and often lack the depth and immediacy required for informed decision-making. EconNews aims to solve this by providing a comprehensive, user-friendly app that consolidates and tailors relevant economic news and data.

## Goals & Success Metrics
### Goals
1. **User Engagement**: Increase daily active users (DAUs) and session duration.
2. **Content Accuracy and Relevance**: Ensure all news and data are accurate and relevant to economic analysts.
3. **API Integration**: Successfully integrate external APIs to provide diverse and up-to-date content.
4. **User Satisfaction**: Achieve a high user satisfaction score through regular feedback and continuous improvement.

### Success Metrics
1. **User Growth**: Aim for 5,000 monthly active users (MAUs) in the first year.
2. **Session Duration**: Average session duration of at least 10 minutes.
3. **Accuracy**: 99% accuracy in news and data sources.
4. **User Feedback**: User satisfaction rating of 4.5/5 based on in-app surveys.

## User Stories
1. **User (Economic Analyst)**
   - As an economic analyst, I want to receive real-time news updates so that I can stay informed about economic trends.
   - As an economic analyst, I want a personalized news feed so that I see content relevant to my interests and work.
   - As an economic analyst, I want to access video news content so that I can consume information in a variety of formats.
   - As an economic analyst, I want to interact with economic trend analysis charts so that I can visualize and understand data better.

2. **System (EconNews App)**
   - As the EconNews app, I should integrate external APIs to fetch and display news and economic data.
   - As the EconNews app, I should provide a user-friendly interface for navigating and engaging with news content.
   - As the EconNews app, I should send push notifications for breaking news and important updates.

## Functional Requirements
1. **News Feed**
   - Display a list of news articles with titles and brief summaries.
   - Allow users to filter news by category (local, global, specific sectors).
   - Provide a personalized news feed based on user preferences and history.

2. **Real-Time Updates**
   - Implement real-time updates for news articles and economic indicators.
   - Send push notifications for breaking news and significant economic events.

3. **Video Content**
   - Support video news content with embedded player and transcript.
   - Provide options to save videos for later viewing.

4. **Economic Trend Analysis**
   - Display interactive charts and graphs for economic indicators.
   - Allow users to customize and save charts for future reference.

5. **User Profile Management**
   - Enable users to create and manage profiles.
   - Allow users to set preferences for news categories and notification settings.

6. **API Integration**
   - Integrate with external news and economic data APIs.
   - Ensure seamless data retrieval and display within the app.

## Non-Functional Requirements
1. **Performance**
   - Ensure a fast loading time for news articles and video content.
   - Maintain high availability and reliability of the app.

2. **Security**
   - Implement secure user authentication and data encryption.
   - Protect user data and privacy in compliance with relevant regulations.

3. **User Experience**
   - Design an intuitive and user-friendly interface.
   - Provide clear and concise instructions for using app features.

4. **Scalability**
   - Design the app to handle a growing user base and increasing data volume.
   - Ensure the system can be easily scaled to accommodate future features and integrations.

## Open Questions
1. **Data Sources**: Which specific APIs will be used for news and economic data?
2. **Monetization Strategy**: How will the app generate revenue (e.g., ads, subscriptions)?
3. **Marketing Plan**: What strategies will be used to attract and retain users?
4. **Localization**: Will the app support multiple languages and regions?
